About us

A few things we believe in

Frictionless payments

Our view is that when payments go fast, businesses grow fast. How can processing payments still take as long as 90 days in such a fast economy? We’re out to change that, for the better.

Flexible experiences

As a consumer, we have so many options when it comes to making a payment. In the B2B world, this isn’t the case. Our goal is to bring the same choice, convenience and freedom to businesses.

Human connections

Tech is at the heart of everything we do, but so are the people behind it. Whether it’s providing support to our customers or recovering payments from debtors, we’re human in everything we do.

Total transparency

We’re true to our word and deliver on our promises. The world of payments can be confusing at the best of times, so we aim to give our customers total transparency. No ifs, ands or buts.
