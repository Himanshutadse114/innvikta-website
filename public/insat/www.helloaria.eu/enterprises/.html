Activate the right lever to preserve your cash flow

Aria helps you manage payments based on your cash position — without disrupting suppliers or adding operational complexity. Reverse factoring, dynamic discounting, deferred payment: three flexible options you can activate straight from your ERP or TMS.
I want to preserve my cash

Preserve your cash without delaying supplier payments

Your suppliers can request immediate payment. You settle with Aria at the agreed due date. Result: you smooth your cash outflows without modifying contractual terms or weakening your supply chain.
I have excess cash to put to work

Turn your cash into a margin driver

You offer early payment in exchange for an automatic discount — calculated based on the date you choose. Result:you improve your purchase margins and monetize your cash effortlessly. Your suppliers get paid faster.
I need to extend my payment terms

Delay your outflows without disrupting your suppliers

Your suppliers are paid upfront. You choose when to reimburse Aria — 30, 60, 90 days or more. Result: you actively reduce your working capital without degrading your partners' experience. They keep their usual cash flow rhythm.
