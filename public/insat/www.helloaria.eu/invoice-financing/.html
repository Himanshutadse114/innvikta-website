Aria stands out thanks to its technology, which enables all invoices to be financed, with no limit on the amount (even your small suppliers can benefit), and direct integration into your software for simplified onboarding.

It can be done in a few weeks depending on the tech team availability and seniority and your workflow complexity. Everything you need to integrate our product is explained into our API documentation. The API supports all payment and invoicing scenarios.

Aria’s invoice financing is based on a receivables assignment. If the invoice owner is the supplier, they will need to agree to the assignment by accepting Aria’s T&C. If you are the invoice owner, there is no need to notify the supplier.

You can request a quote to know instantly if the buyer is eligible to deferred payment. After a loan is requested you receive the confirmation and money is transferred to the supplier within 24 hours.

Trust that all invoices will be financed. So far, we’ve been able to offer close to 99% acceptance for corporations and 90% for SMEs.
